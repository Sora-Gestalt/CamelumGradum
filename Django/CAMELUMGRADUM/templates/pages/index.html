About CamelumGradum

CamelumGradum, the Latin name for "camel step," was created by Eng. Nawaf Abdullah Al Taleb in 2017 in Riyadh. CamelumGradum is a specialist and professional cafe known well in France (Paris), Italy (Vatican City/Rome), Netherlands (Amsterdam), and Turkey (Istanbul).

Our story begins in 2016 when Eng. Nawaf was studying Software Engineering. Nawaf, a coffee enthusiast and professional barista, decided to establish the Saudi brand CamelumGradum. Our beans are sourced from specialist farms in Columbia, Costa Rica, Ethiopia, Yemen, Ghana, Saudi Arabia, and France.

Have a great journey at our Cafe!
